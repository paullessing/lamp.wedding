Providing the weather is good, we're planning to have a picnic in Victoria Park in East London.

Everyone is welcome, including kids and everyone who couldn't make it on Saturday!

When?

Come whenever you feel like, leave when you're bored.
We'll be aiming to arrive between 12 noon and 1pm and should be there most of the afternoon.
(Depending on how excited/tired/danced out we are it might be a little earlier or later!)
