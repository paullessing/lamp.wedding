By Car

If you're driving, you can use the car park at the location.
Unfortunately we have to completely clear the venue before 4am, so you will be unable to leave it there overnight.
If you are staying late (and we hope so!), we recommend taking public transport instead.

By Public Transport

The closest station is Canning Town, which is served by the Jubilee Line and DLR.

The Jubilee Line runs the Night Tube, so you can take it back home afterwards too!

By Helicopter or Private Jet

You may be able land your private aircraft at London City Airport and take the DLR to the venue,
although you will probably have to leave the aircraft at the airport overnight.
Contact the airport directly to enquire about a space.
